You are invited to attend our upcoming webinar, "How to Mobilize your EHR Information Quickly and Securely." The webinar begins at 12:00PM ET, Tuesday, March 21st.

Why You Should Attend:
With the adoption of electronic records, healthcare has been digitized. However, the data is too difficult to access. Join Niko Skievaski, co-founder and president of Redox (the healthcare API company), and Jikku Venkat, VP of products at Kinvey (the leading HIPAA compliant mobile development platform company), to learn how to strip away all archaic complexity and provide a familiar, modern API. Together, we enable your developers to code once and connect to all your EHR systems and transform this information into secure and engaging patient and provider app experiences - quickly.

What You'll Learn:

Why your app developers don’t need to know anything about HL7, FHIR, CDA, CCD, X12, NCPDP, DICOM, EDI to create modern app experiences.
Why you don’t need for the world to change; everything you need to innovate is already in place.
How to build apps that will scale across your enterprise and beyond.
That HIPAA compliant apps can be easy to build - you can start prototyping today.
